Frequently asked questions

Can I make additional payments or pay off my loan early?

Yes, you can make additional payments or pay off your loan early without penalty. This can help you save on interest charges and become debt-free sooner. Contact us to discuss your repayment options.

What are the maximum loan amounts available at Whatfix Bank?

Our personal loans range from $2,000 to $500,000, depending on your creditworthiness and financial situation. Business loans can go up to $5 million. The exact amount depends on your income, credit score, and ability to repay.

Do you offer refinancing options for existing loans?

Yes, we offer competitive refinancing options for both personal and business loans. Refinancing can help you secure better interest rates, lower monthly payments, or access additional funds. Speak with our advisors to explore your options.

What banking services are available beyond loans?

Whatfix Bank offers comprehensive banking services including savings accounts, checking accounts, investment products, mortgage services, business banking, and wealth management. We're your complete financial partner.

When is the best time to apply for a loan?

The best time to apply is when you have stable income, good credit standing, and a clear purpose for the funds. We recommend applying at least 30 days before you need the money to ensure smooth processing and optimal terms.

Need assistance with your banking needs?

Our expert financial advisors are ready to help you find the perfect banking solution for your unique situation.
