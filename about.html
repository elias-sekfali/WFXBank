Welcome to Whatfix Bank

Whatfix Bank is a comprehensive demonstration platform designed to showcase the capabilities of modern digital banking solutions. Our platform provides a realistic banking experience for training, testing, and demonstration purposes.

We offer a complete range of banking services including personal loans, current accounts, credit cards, savings products, and mortgage solutions. Our user-friendly interface and advanced features make banking simple and accessible for everyone.
